About FPPA

FPPA, which stands for "For Pickleball Player Action," is dedicated to promoting Pickleball and bringing people together to play the sport.

In addition, we are proud to support Yu-Chieh Hsieh, also known as the Pickleball Asia Queen. We are thrilled to announce that we will be donating 10% of our profits to support her training and international competitions.
