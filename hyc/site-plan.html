Home page

About us: Yu-Chieh Hsieh is a brave girl from Taiwan and a professional tennis and pickleball player. We are committed to supporting her to showcase her best performance without any worries in international tournaments and cheering for her dreams and achievements. Here, we will follow her events and life together, and support her through purchasing pickleball products. Yu-Chieh's words: I really want more people to experience the charm of pickleball and hope to be with a group of friends who love this sport. In my travels around the world for competitions and training, I have experienced many setbacks and difficulties due to insufficient resources. But I still firmly love my sports career and look forward to everyone's support. Whether it is cheering for me in competitions, following my updates on social media, or even supporting me by purchasing FPPA merchandise, it can give me strength and help me achieve higher goals. At this moment, I may be on my way to training or on the competition field. Your support will give me more opportunities to stand on the podium. Personal social media: Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=100063575327746&mibextid=ZbWKwL IG: https://www.instagram.com/yuchieh.hsieh/ YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/@sportsofyu-chiehhsieh7529 *Yu-Chieh represents her country around the world and looks forward to having you accompany her! *
